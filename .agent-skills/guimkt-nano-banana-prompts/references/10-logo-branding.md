## Prompts Principais

### 3D Metallic Logo Render

**Descrição:** Logos de marcas renderizados como objetos 3D metálicos.

```
Brand logos rendered as highly detailed 3D metallic objects 
against clean white background. Each logo features brushed metal texture 
with subtle reflections and scratches, emphasizing modern corporate identity.

Technical: Studio lighting, product photography style, 
ultra-realistic material rendering.
```

**Fonte:** [@AmirMushich](https://x.com/AmirMushich/status/2000614012422136108)

### 3D Glass Logo Render

**Descrição:** Logos renderizados como objetos de vidro translúcido.

```
Brand logos rendered as translucent, glossy glass objects. 
Each logo rests on vibrant, monochromatic background in shades 
matching brand colors. Clean and modern 3D design aesthetic 
with subtle shadows and highlights.

Style: Product photography, minimalist, premium finish.
```

**Fonte:** [@AmirMushich](https://x.com/AmirMushich/status/1995542490049290680)

### Monogram Logo Design

**Descrição:** Logo estilo monograma calligráfico elegante.

```
Prominent white calligraphic monogram or stylized letter 
against bold [brand color] background. Below the elegant emblem, 
display the brand logo with distinctive elements.

Style: Luxury branding, heritage aesthetic, premium typography.
```

**Fonte:** [@youraipulse](https://x.com/youraipulse/status/2006016437354148100)

### Logo Animation Concept

**Descrição:** Conceito para animação de logo 3D.

```
3D logo reveal animation concept:
- Logo emerges from [material: liquid metal, particles, smoke]
- Camera orbits around the logo
- Dynamic lighting with [accent colors]
- Final frame: logo centered with subtle ambient motion

Technical: Product visualization, cinema 4D aesthetic, 
motion graphics ready.
```
