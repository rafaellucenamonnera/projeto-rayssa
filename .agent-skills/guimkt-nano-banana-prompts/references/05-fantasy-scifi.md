## Sci-Fi Film Aesthetic

### X-Wing Pilot Portrait (Star Wars Style)

**Descrição:** Retrato cinematográfico de piloto sci-fi no estilo Rogue One/Andor.

```
Role: You are a world-class unit still photographer for high-budget sci-fi films.
Generate a photorealistic cinematic portrait in the visual tone of "Rogue One" or "Andor".

TOP PRIORITY - IDENTITY PRESERVATION:
- Use exact person from reference photo as subject
- Maintain exact facial structure: nose, eyes, jawline, cheekbones, mouth
- Preserve skin tone and natural skin details (pores, freckles, moles, scars)
- Treat subject as a real actor placed into a new scene

AESTHETIC REQUIREMENTS:
- Photorealistic film still with tangible textures (heavy canvas, scratched plastic, 
  grease stains, industrial metal)
- Cinematic lighting and organic film grain
- Grounded, war-movie atmosphere

STYLE TARGET - THE REBELLION (Rogue One Era):
- Gritty, desaturated, "boots on the ground" military sci-fi aesthetic
- Palette: Industrial greys, dirty orange, olive drab, cold hangar lighting
- Vibe: Determined, weary, functional, ready for combat

SCENE SETTING:
- Cinematic medium shot in busy Rebel Hangar (e.g., Yavin 4)
- Background: Blurred mechanics, astromech droids, parts of X-Wing fighter
- Action: Subject stands holding rebel pilot helmet under one arm

WARDROBE (X-Wing Pilot):
- Flight Suit: Iconic orange, heavy fire-resistant canvas, worn and grease-stained
- Vest: White flak vest with life-support box on chest
- Details: Hoses, heavy black gloves, leg flares

LIGHTING: Industrial hangar lighting, mixed color temperatures
CAMERA: Arri Alexa 65 / 70s Hybrid aesthetic, fine film grain
```
