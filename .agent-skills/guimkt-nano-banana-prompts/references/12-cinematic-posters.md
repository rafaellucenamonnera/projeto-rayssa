## Prompts Principais

### Character + Cartoon Duo Poster

**Descrição:** Pessoa real ao lado de personagem de desenho animado em estilo poster promocional.

```
Ultra-high-resolution 8K studio portrait featuring a real human 
standing beside a stylized cartoon character of choice, 
full-body composition, both subjects clearly visible and centered.

Human subject: young adult with natural smile, relaxed confident posture, 
photorealistic skin texture, detailed hair strands, wearing modern casual clothing.

Cartoon character: [INSERT CHARACTER NAME], fully animated and stylized, 
vibrant saturated colors, smooth surface texture, expressive large eyes, 
friendly confident expression, interacting naturally with human subject.

Lighting: soft professional studio lighting, subtle rim light 
separating subjects from background.

Background: clean minimal studio backdrop, solid color or smooth gradient.

Style: hyper-detailed photorealistic human + high-quality 3D cartoon render, 
commercial advertising look, premium finish.
```

**Fonte:** [@meng_dagg695](https://x.com/meng_dagg695/status/2005988472369398183)

### UFC Walkout Cinematic

**Descrição:** Cena cinematográfica de entrada de lutador no túnel.

```
Cinematic UFC photo - tunnel walkout scene featuring [FIGHTER NAME], 
his crew in specific hoodies, paparazzi with flashing cameras. 
Use best practices of cinematographer and photographer.

Style: High contrast, dramatic lighting, motion blur on crowd, 
sharp focus on main subject. Anamorphic lens flares, 
gritty documentary aesthetic.
```

**Fonte:** [@tyler_agg](https://x.com/tyler_agg/status/1970192360102633768)

### Giant Object in City

**Descrição:** Objeto do cotidiano transformado em monumento urbano.

```
Transform [EVERYDAY OBJECT] into a massive real-world monument. 
Surface materials are physically accurate, with visible wear, scratches, dust, 
and scale references like people and vehicles. 

Shot from low-angle cinematic perspective, realistic daylight, 
ultra-detailed textures.

Examples:
- Giant pencil being assembled in city center
- Massive coffee cup as public installation
- Enormous sneaker as urban sculpture
```

### Movie Poster Template

**Descrição:** Template para criação de key art cinematográfico.

```
Cinematic movie poster composition:

Hero shot of [SUBJECT] in dramatic [ACTION/POSE].
Background: [EPIC ENVIRONMENT with scale elements].
Lighting: [DRAMATIC LIGHTING SCHEME - usually low-key with rim lights].

Color grade: [TEAL AND ORANGE / DESATURATED / HIGH CONTRAST].
Typography placeholder at top and bottom.

Style: AAA blockbuster marketing, IMAX-ready, 
photorealistic with subtle color grading.
```
