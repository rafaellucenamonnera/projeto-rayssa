### Rainy Night Market Alley

**Descrição:** Render 3D de beco de mercado noturno com atmosfera cinematográfica.

```
Detailed 3D render of a bustling narrow night market alley, 
with rain-wet cobblestone streets reflecting the glow of vibrant lanterns. 
The scene features various food stalls, people with umbrellas, 
and traditional buildings under a moody, overcast sky.

Technical: Cinematic lighting, volumetric fog, puddle reflections, 
steam from food stalls, soft bokeh on distant lights.
```
